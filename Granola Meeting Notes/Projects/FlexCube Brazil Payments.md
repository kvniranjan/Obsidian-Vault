# FlexCube Brazil Payments

## Overview
End-to-end payment processing for Brazil on FlexCube — covering consolidated posting, earmarking workflows, capacity planning (200+ TPS targets), Green Zone booking, and seven-day sweep processing. Key topics include the earmark-then-post flow, book-to-book 4-leg posting, advanced earmarking for high-volume clients (Uber, PayPal, Rappi), and weekend transaction handling.
