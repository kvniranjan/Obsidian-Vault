# GL Reclassification

## Overview
Implementing an interim GL approach to resolve toxic combination issues at FFS side where loan asset reporting from DDA creates sync problems. Involves credit account class reclassification for OED, a new interim GL account (299), and dimension configuration analysis. Brazil relies on common balance only (unlike Argentina/Canada GL system).
