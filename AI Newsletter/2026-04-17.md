This earnings cycle marks the clearest signal yet that the narrative has shifted from "AI augments workers" to "AI replaces measurable categories of work" — and that banks are willing to say so publicly.

### CaixaBank Launches GlobalView: AI-Augmented Wealth Aggregator Built on BlackRock Aladdin
**Source:** [CaixaBank Newsroom](https://www.caixabank.com/en/headlines/news/caixabank-private-banking-is-launching-a-new-service-unique-in-spain-that-combines-agility-in-portfolio-construction-with-maximum-personalization-in-advisory)  •  **Date:** April 16, 2026

CaixaBank's Wealth Management division launched GlobalView, described as the first financial position aggregator in Spain that consolidates clients' cross-institution holdings under a unified, AI-enhanced analytical framework. Built in collaboration with Flanks (a Bank of Spain-regulated wealthtech) for automated data collection and standardisation, and integrated with BlackRock's Aladdin Wealth™ platform for portfolio analytics, GlobalView delivers daily-updated unified net worth views and aligned risk analysis across all a client's custodian relationships. The initial rollout targets Global Wealth clients with independent advisory contracts holding more than €4 million in assets. Beyond the technology, the strategic significance is regulatory: GlobalView is positioned as ahead of the EU's FIDA regulation (Financial Data Access, effective 2027), which will mandate broad financial data portability across the European system. For wealth managers globally, CaixaBank's move illustrates how open-data infrastructure enables genuinely personalised AI-driven advisory at scale — and how front-runners are building that capability now, ahead of the regulatory mandate.
